Multicolour — Dual-Accent Editorial Systems

Six editorial palette systems, each with two distinct accent colours rather than a single monochrome accent. In each system, Accent 1 is structural (section labels, company names, borders) and Accent 2 is editorial (kicker, dates, links). Dark and light variants shown for each.
