Additive — Signature Colour

Amber/cream stays intact across the entire site. One signature colour is applied only to identity touchpoints: the icon/favicon, hero kicker, scroll progress bar, and OG image stripe. Amber remains the editorial accent for dates, labels, and links. The kicker and EQ. chip below show the signature colour; everything else is unchanged.

Bold — Color as Background

The accent color is the page. These palettes paint large structural areas with color rather than reserving it for accents. Each bold variant is paired with its pale tint below — background panels scroll at a different rate than the content for a parallax depth effect.
